As his alias is indicating, Max Prosa is a singer-songwriter straight from the book. Being far ahead, he skipped a year in school, but then abandoned his physics and philosophy studies to conquer the Berlin singer-songwriter scene. In 2011 at the age of 22 he gained the attention of one of his most successful peers, Clueso, who hired him as a support for his tour. Right afterwards, his first album „Die Phantasie wird siegen“ („Imagination will prevail“) was released and became an immense success.

After he released two more albums, he refused to renew his contract with his label Columbia Records/Sony. Instead, he wanted to be free of any meddling by a label in order to please an audience that was ascertained by market researchers. So he started a crowd founding campaign, collected 23.000 EUR and produced his fourth album in his own living room. This is just a hint to the level of authenticity that you will find throughout his deep and rich music.

I got to know him through the same radio show, that I wrote about in my first article about AnnenMayKantereit. If anyone deserves to be called the „young German Bob Dylan“, it is definitely Max Prosa. I saw him right afterwards in 2012 in a really small concert in Frankfurt and have listened to his music ever since.

It was incredibly hard for me to choose just one song to present here, but I decided to go with „Flügel“ („Wings“), which is his first single (2011) from his first album, written at an age of 22. I remember very well, how the earth stood still for the three minutes, when he played "Flügel" at the concert. It is just great on so many levels. I hope you’ll enjoy it.
