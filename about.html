about

Only when a great tune comes together with great lyrics, a song can unfold its entire power. If you don’t understand the lyrics, you might like the melody, but it takes much more to really carry you away.

Since German is not a wide-spread language, most people are oblivious to German music. And even those people, who have learned to speak decent German, will have difficulties to understand and appreciate the more intricate lyrics, since the language is often playfully modified and many songs are packed with cultural references, that are incomprehensible for non-native speakers.

On train ride to berlin, I want to share my passion for German singer/songwriter music. I’m going to present songs and artists and I will translate the lyrics and explain things that go beyond the Google translater experience. Follow this blog if you are interested in German music and pop culture.
